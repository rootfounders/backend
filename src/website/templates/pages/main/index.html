Introducing RootFounders

A build-in-public founder community designed for idea validation and collaborative development. The platform offers a space where founders can connect with like-minded individuals, share progress transparently, and receive feedback to refine their ideas.

Explore projects

Validate idea

Sharing your idea on RootFounders platform will help you acheive feasibility through insight from comments, votes, endorsement and even tips.

Find cofounders

Discover interest on your ideas as interested indiviuals can easily send proposal to build together with you. This way you build a team of likeminded, experienced individuals – the first step to building a successful project.

Community engagement made easy

RootFounders is built to support ideas easily with the implementation of platform credits. Credits act as a spendable token to support other builders' ideas and build thriving communities.
